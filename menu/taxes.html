Tax Services

Navigating the ever-changing landscape of tax regulations can be a daunting task, but with MANFISER INC. you have a partner committed to crafting strategic tax solutions. Our experts delve deep into your financial situation, identifying opportunities for optimization and compliance. Whether you're facing complex tax challenges or seeking proactive tax planning, we tailor our services to meet your specific needs. Trust us to guide you through the intricate world of taxes, ensuring you maximize benefits and stay on the right side of regulations.

Business Tax Essentials

The Business Tax Essentials package is perfect for sole proprietors and single-member S corporations just starting out. We'll help you navigate Schedule C (Form 1040) for sole proprietors and Form 1120S for S corporations. No need to decipher tax code – we'll handle the filing and ensure accuracy. Minimize your tax burden and maximize peace of mind.

Business Tax Growth

The Business Tax Growth package is ideal for entrepreneurs with sole proprietorships, S corporations, and LLCs. It includes all the benefits of the Essentials package, plus:
  • Tax handling for your LLC, regardless of the specific business structure.
  • Expert analysis of your finances and smart tax planning strategies to maximize your deductions and credits, saving you money.

Business Tax Enterprise

The Business Tax Enterprise package is designed for complex business structures like corporations and partnerships. It builds from the Growth package, plus:
  • Comprehensive representation during IRS audits and inquiries
  • Customized dashboards and reporting for complete financial transparency
  • A dedicated tax advisor who can guide you through complex tax situations and help you make informed decisions for your business.
