Payroll Services

Timely and accurate payroll is not just a legal requirement but also a crucial factor in maintaining a motivated and satisfied team. At MANFISER INC. we take the hassle out of payroll management, ensuring that your employees are paid accurately and on time. From calculating wages and deductions to handling tax withholdings, we streamline the entire payroll process, allowing you to foster a positive work environment while we take care of the details.

Monthly Payroll Essentials

Simplify your small business payroll with our Essentials package. Ideal for teams under 5, we handle weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly or monthly payruns, ensuring accurate employee payments and hassle-free payroll tax submissions. Enjoy peace of mind and focus on what matters – growing your business.

Monthly Payroll Growth

Automate your payroll and beat compliance headaches with the Growth package. Made for companies with up to 10 employees, we streamline your monthly reporting, including Workers' Comp reports and audits, EDD unemployment and disability letters, and IRS payroll tax communications. Take the stress out of monthly tasks and dedicate your energy to scaling your business.

Monthly Payroll Enterprise

Unleash the full power of comprehensive payroll management with our Enterprise package. Designed for companies with up to 20 employees, it combines all the features of Essentials and Growth, plus dedicated support and seamless integration with your existing business systems. Gain complete control, optimize efficiency, and focus on strategic growth while we handle the complexities of your payroll.
